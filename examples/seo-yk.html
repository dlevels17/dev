Search Engine Optimization

SEO

One of the most integral parts of digital marketing is SEO. Organic search results is as competitive as ever and with an overwhelming majority of visitors visiting the first page of the results. Multiple studies have shown not being on the first page makes your presence on the web relatively invisible. As part of our website traffic analysis, we will run your website through our thorough SEO audit either before if you have an existing site or after if we're building it from the ground up. Once we have a baseline we run through our state of the art SEO process steps to build a fully optimal website that search engines can find.

Reseach Process

We find the best keywords for your business to further elevate your traffic. We take a data driven approach to find and use the most optimal keywords for your website. Then we will find the relevant links to build your authority. We strictly use white hat SEO techniques to improve your rankings giving you the peace of mind that you will not be penalized by search engines.

Technical Optimization & Mobile Responsiveness

We will analyze all the technical factors in SEO and make sure your website is up to par with your competitors. This includes to optimize your website for mobile use and speed performance. The majority of searches are now done on mobile and this trend is expected to continue. Making your website mobile responsive is now a necessity for your search rankings.

Implementation

Once we have determined the most optimal keywords and links we will implement a strategy to drive more traffic to your site and increase your conversions. This involves continuous testing and reworking your site in order to reach your target audience. Building your reputation through link building will further increase your authority on the web and flow more traffic your way. We will implement the keywords and link from our research and put them on your site to start working driving more traffic.
