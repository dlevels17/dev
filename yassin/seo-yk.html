Search Engine Optimization

SEO

Get your business found not the other way around! It’s too easy for your business get lost in search engine results. If your website is not currently optimized for everyone that is searching your product or service online you’re leaving potential growth opportunities behind. No two businesses are alike, that’s why we will help cater and build an SEO campaign unique to your business. Let us help you reach your goals one step at a time.

Optimization Process

One of the most integral parts of any digital marketing strategy is search engine optimization. Organic search results is as competitive as ever and with an overwhelming majority of visitors visiting just the first page of the results. Studies have shown not being on the page one makes your presence on the web relatively invisible. As part of our website traffic analysis, we will run your website through our thorough SEO audit test. Once we have a baseline we run through our state of the art optimization process with steps to fully build an optimal website for organic search.

Data Research

We find the best keywords and links for your business to further elevate your traffic. We take a data driven approach to find and use the most optimal keywords and links for your website. Unlikely some of our competitors we don’t believe in cutting corners by doing quick fixes for short term gain. We take the long term approach and that’s why we strictly use white hat SEO techniques to improve your rankings without taking the risk that your search ranking might be penalized in the future by using other methods.

Technical Optimization & Mobile Responsiveness

We will analyze your technical SEO and measure your website’s performance. This includes to optimize your website for mobile use and speed performance. The majority of searches are now done on mobile and this trend is expected to continue. Making your website mobile responsive is now a necessity for your search rankings.

Implementation

Once we have determined the most optimal keywords and links we will implement a SEO strategy to drive more traffic to your site and increase your conversions. This involves continuous testing and reworking your site in order to reach your target audience.

Local SEO Strategy

We will take into account all the local SEO ranking factors to create your local presence online.
